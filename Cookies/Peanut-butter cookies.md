Peanut-Butter Cookies

¼ cup shortening + ¼ cup butter

½ cup peanut butter

½ cup granulated sugar

½ cup packed brown sugar

1 egg

1-1/2 cup flour

½ tsp baking powder

¾ tsp baking soda

¼ tsp salt

Bake at 375 F for 10-12 minutes

Drizzle or drip in chocolate, roll in peanuts, or sprinkle with sea salt
